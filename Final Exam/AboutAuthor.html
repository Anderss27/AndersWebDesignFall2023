About Dr. Seuss

Dr Seuss is a two time Emmy Award, a Peabody Award, and three Academy Awardwinner. He is most famous for his rhyming and creativity in his books. He created Cat in the Hat, Green Eggs and Ham, and One fish Two fish Red fish Blue fish.

Dr. Seuss has inspired so many authors to write children books and to be creative and be yourself. He truly is one of the most famous, inspiring, and accomplished authors to live.

Before Dr. Suess was famous he was studying English literature at the University of Oxford
